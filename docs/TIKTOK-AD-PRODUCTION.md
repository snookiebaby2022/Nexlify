# TikTok ad production — Nexlify

Production pack for two vertical ads driving traffic to **https://nexlify.live** (license) with proof at **https://panel.nexlify.live** (demo: `admin` / `admin123`).

**Brand:** dark navy `#080d18`, cyan `#22d3ee`, orange CTA `#f97316`  
**Export:** 9:16 · 1080×1920 · 30fps · H.264 · ~8–12 Mbps · AAC 48kHz  
**Safe zones:** keep text/logos inside center 80%; captions above bottom 250px (TikTok UI overlap)

---
