## Why no VO

- 80%+ of TikTok views start muted — text carries the story.
- Beat-synced cuts keep pace without narration.
- Pair with caption pack in `PRODUCTION-GUIDE.md` (Ad C).

## Editing rules

- **Every cut on the beat** (~0.47s at 128 BPM half-notes, or ~0.94s full bars).
- Text animations: pop-in 0.15s, no long typewriter effects.
- Screen record at **native 1080p**; crop center for mobile if recording ultrawide.
- Optional: 1.05× speed on table scrolls (CapCut speed ramp).

## A/B hook variants

Swap 0:00.0–0:01.0 text + visual hook only. See `PRODUCTION-GUIDE.md` Hooks 3–5.
